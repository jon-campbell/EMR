Walking stick with magical properties.

  • Mandrake/Iron compound

NOTE:
Using weapon in close proximity to target is hazardous.

         
WAND HISTORY:

Wands were fabled devices of sorcerers and wizards during medieval times. No weapon of such magical properties had been scientifically proven to exist until your visit to the Camelot period.

The magic staff used by Merlin supports two primary types of spells that can be used by anyone who holds the staff, whether they are trained in sorcerery or not. The primary spell is a ball of fire which catapults to distances up to 300 yards. The fireball is extremely potent, and will incinerate those in the vicinity of its landing zone. Archers are extremely susceptable to fireballs given their smaller size and flammable attire.

The secondary spell is a freezing spiral which defies gravity. Although the spiral moves very slowly, there is no limit as to how far it can travel. Once it reaches its target, it dispurses into freezing gases. Most organic forms are paralyzed by the subzero temperatures of these gases. The targeting error rate of the freeze spell is poor in comparison to the fireball, but the magic staff can emit three spirals for every one fireball. A room filled with freezing gases is difficult to escape.

Once the magic of the staff is used up, the staff is deemed useless. A new staff must be found to obtain these spells again.

The staff is also known to possess powers to perform other spells when the surrounding energy aura supports the spell. Such spells that are known include Giants Stomp, Raise the Dead, Teleportation, and Fire Storm.
