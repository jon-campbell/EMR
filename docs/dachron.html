<Event Log 672.194>
Dachron Minigun

Lightweight, rapid-fire assault rifle.

  • 13 barrels
  • 4 chambers
  • Lightweight alloy

NOTE:
Secondary trigger empties all 13 barrels of a single chamber simultaneously

         
DACHRON HISTORY:

Following the first successful light speed space exploration mission of 2180, COPRE (Consortium of Private Research Enterprises) began an intense study of rare minerals collected from the newly discovered planet, Dachronia. Dachronian technology became viable in 2204 when scientists discovered new properties in fluid mechanics involving the use of this new mineral.

When the DOD (Department of Defense) ascertained the vast importance of this new discovery and the impact it would have on weapons technology, they immediately seized half of the Dachronia minerals surplus for secret government projects. Working with LockMart-Boeing-DuPont engineers, the DOD developed a new metal from these minerals that was lightweight, yet more durable than the hardest titanium alloy. It also emitted very low friction heat. Taking advantage of this metal's unique properties, scientists developed new high speed, high powered handheld weapons.

By the year 2215, the government had perfected a highpowered rapid fire assault rifle: the Dachron minigun.

The Dachron minigun is an extraordinarily volatile weapon. It's creation was inspired by the weapons known as miniguns that were used during the latter part of the twentieth century. It was in the year 2032 when the United Nations "Peaceable World Constitution" banned the use of such weapons. The basic design philosophy of the Dachron followed that of its predecessors: The more firepower available to a soldier, the more likely the enemy would suffer greater casualties. The Dachron, however, has an advantage over its primitive counterparts. Where the minigun had only one firing chamber, the Dachron contains thirteen. The gun consists of 13 revolving barrels. With 4 chambers per barrel, the Dachron is capable of a full 52 rounds of shots before reload. The two revolutionary aspects of the gun, however, are its very light weight given its fire power, and its secondary trigger mechanism which allows all 13 barrels to expend a shot simultaneously. This is an extremely lethal punch. It uses ammunition much faster than the primary trigger, and as such it is advised that it not be used unless under extreme duress. In general, one Dachron minigun is issued per platoon.

As a matter of history, this secondary burst shot would prove to be highly effective in the WorldSoft coup of 2258. Rebel executives obtained several Dachron miniguns from the government and staged a coup unmatched by any in history. Kahnene Forraje, WorldSoft's leader at the time, controlled an arsenal comparable to a second world country, including a massive horde of self-destructing Forraje clones. With one burst shot from the Dachron minigun, however, an entire room of Forraje clones could be destroyed. Eventually Forraje's army was defeated, and new power took over WorldSoft. Forraje himself was hunted down and executed with an ordinary Snyper pistol.
